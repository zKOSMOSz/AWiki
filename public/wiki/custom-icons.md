### Custom Icons

To use a custom icon, use its name in the `iconName` field.

**Available Custom Icons:**

-   `InfoIcon`
-   `BookIcon`
-   `UserPlusIcon`
-   `BeakerIcon`
-   `WineIcon`
-   `DocumentTextIcon`
-   `MicrophoneIcon`
-   `FolderIcon`
-   `ClockIcon`
-   `ShieldCheckIcon`
-   `BuildingLibraryIcon`
